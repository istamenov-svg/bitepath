Your calories have a geography

BitePath connects what you eat to where you are — surfacing the right meal at the right place throughout your day.

Plan your meals in the morning

Set your daily calorie goal, food budget, and locations. Tell the app where you'll be — home, work, or on the go — and BitePath builds your day around it. One tap to start planning.

Find what fits nearby

See restaurants near your current location, sorted by distance, price, and how many menu items fit your remaining calories and budget. Every option is filtered to what actually works for you right now.

Pick the right bite

Every menu item shows full nutrition details — calories, protein, fat, carbs, and price. No guessing. Pick what fits your goals and your wallet, with confidence.

Find and track every meal

See your progress in real-time. Track calories consumed, budget spent, and protein hit across breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Context-aware nudges help you stay on track for your last meal of the day.

How BitePath works

1

Set your goals

Choose your daily calorie target and food budget. Set your home and work addresses so BitePath knows where to search.

2

Pick your meals

For each meal, see nearby restaurants with items that fit your remaining calories and budget. Choose what works.

3

Stay on track

Track your progress throughout the day. BitePath adjusts what's available based on what you've already eaten.
