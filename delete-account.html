Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 14, 2026

You can delete your BitePath account and all associated data directly from within the app. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the BitePath app and sign in to your account.
  2. On the "Plan Your Day" screen, tap the settings gear icon in the top-right corner.
  3. In the settings menu that appears, tap "Delete Account".
  4. A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to confirm the deletion. Tap "Delete" to proceed.
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted and you will be returned to the landing page.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  1. Your user profile (name, email address, display name)
  2. Your saved home and work addresses
  3. Your calorie and budget preferences
  4. Your meal planning history (daily plans, selected meals)
  5. Your authentication credentials

All data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. There is no additional retention period.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, or if you need assistance, contact us at support@bitepath.app with the email address associated with your account and we will process your deletion request within 48 hours.
